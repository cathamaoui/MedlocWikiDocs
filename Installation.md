# 📥 Installation & Getting Started

Welcome to Medloc. Follow these steps to join the Moncton healthcare network.

## 📱 For Nurses
1. **Download the App:** Search for "Medloc" on the iOS App Store or Google Play Store.
2. **Create Account:** Click "Sign Up" and verify your phone number for 📲 SMS shift alerts.
3. **Complete Profile:** Upload your nursing credentials and set your real-time availability.

## 🏥 For Healthcare Facilities
1. **Visit the Web Portal:** Go to [medloc.ca](https://medloc.ca) to access the employer dashboard.
2. **Registration:** Create a facility profile and link your existing scheduling system.
3. **Verification:** Once our team verifies your clinic, you can begin posting shifts instantly.
