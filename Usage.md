# 📱 Usage Guide

Medloc is designed to be simple for both healthcare facilities and nursing staff.

### For Clinics: Posting a Shift
1. Log into the **Medloc Dashboard**.
2. Click **"Post New Shift"**.
3. Enter the date, time, and hourly rate.
4. Hit **"Broadcast"** to alert all matching nurses in the Moncton area.

### For Nurses: Accepting a Shift
1. You will receive an **SMS notification** when a shift matches your profile.
2. Reply **"YES"** to the text message to instantly claim the shift.
3. Check your **"My Shifts"** tab in the app for location details and check-in instructions.
